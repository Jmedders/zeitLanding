Fathoms

Emotionally charged words known as Fathoms. Fathoms are a starting point to inspire creativity; from a place of structure comes absolute freedom. The Editorial team will create seasonal Fathoms.

Users can navigate through stories filtered by individual Fathoms to discover the inventions of others.



Some Fathoms include:<Tail>, <Zombie>, <Alien>, <Robot>, <Demon>, <Kingdom>, <Lipstick>, <Rainbow>, <Basement>, <River>, <Scissors>, <Crystal-ball>, <Cherub>, <Mirror>, <Wheatfield>...
